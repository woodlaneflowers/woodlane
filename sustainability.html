Sustainability

Locally grown flowers are better for the environment. Why import flowers that have been flown for miles, lost any scent and often have a poor vase life. We all know that the flowers long journey is a primary cause of climate change.

By growing flowers locally, we are helping diversity of insect and wildlife, while making the landscape look beautiful. Whatâ€™s not to like!

I use plastic free packaging, using plant based cellophane for aqua packed flowers, making the bouquet wrapping fully compostable. My arrangements are created with the use of a flower frog, chicken wire or Agra wool. I avoid using floristry foam and am keen to see the floristry trade move away from this plastic filled product.

I can provide a small posy in a recycle jam jar or a recycled tin which I cover with beautiful fabric.
