Wedding venue flowers

Every wedding is unique, and I can provide flowers that work for different venues, styles and budgets. I love meeting couples to fully appreciate what look you'd like to create, and what would work well for your wedding venue.

The beauty of locally grown flowers is capturing the seasonality of your big day. If however, you have a particular flower that you adore, I will endeavour to source it for you to work within my arrangement.

I can make small table arrangements, large urns, decorative arches, and stunning bridal bouquets to set your wedding apart.

If you would prefer to arrange the flowers yourself I can be on hand, with buckets and snips and help you pick your own flowers. I will be able to help guide you with colours and flowers which complement each other and offer advice on how best to condition your flowers.

You can combine DIY flowers with some of the key elements, such as a bridal bouquet and button holes.

Your flowers will be ready the day before for collection, or I can deliver on the morning of your wedding, allowing you to focus on enjoying every moment.

Having a winter wedding? I can make dried flower arrangements that allow you to enjoy them for months to come.
